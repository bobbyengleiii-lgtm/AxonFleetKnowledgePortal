# Proper Grounding and how to test if it is a good ground

## 1) Client Issue Description
If a ground is installed with having high resistance it can cause numorous issues with Fleet3

## 2) Observed Symptoms
Clarified/asked team to ensure everyone knows how to properly test if it’s a good ground (Using Multimeter set it to ohms and put one lead on your ground stud and the other lead on the chassis of the vehicle), you should have less then .03 ohms reading to be a proper ground if the number is higher then 1 then do not use this as your ground for Fleet3.
